Team & billing rates

Each person's hourly rate is used to value the hours they save. Changing a rate only affects entries logged from then on — past entries keep the rate that was in effect when they were logged, so historical totals stay auditable.

Settings

Backup & restore

Everything lives in this browser only. Export a backup to move your log to another machine, or send it to your team lead to combine everyone's numbers.

How the numbers are built

For every task you log:
• Hours saved = time without Claude − time with Claude (never below zero).
• Value = hours saved × that person's bill rate.
• Leverage = total hours without ÷ total hours with — how many hours of work each hour in Claude returns.
Day, week, and month totals are just sums of the entries that fall in each window. These are estimates you enter, not measurements — keep them honest and they make a defensible case for the value Claude returns to the firm.
